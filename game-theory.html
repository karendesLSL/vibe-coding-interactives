Step 2: What are "Payoffs"?

The numbers in the boxes aren't always just dollars. They represent Utilityâ€”a measure of how "happy" or "satisfied" a player is with an outcome.

Why no units? Because payoffs represent a ranking of outcomes. A payoff of "10" is simply better than "5." It could mean 10 apples, 10 dollars, or 10 minutes of saved time.
10, 5

When you see (10, 5):

  • The First Number (10) is always for the Row Player (You).
  • The Second Number (5) is always for the Column Player (Them).
